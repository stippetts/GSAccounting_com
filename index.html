Good questions

Before you call, you might be wondering…

I'm switching from another accountant — is that complicated?

Not on your end. With your okay, we request your prior records and handle the handoff. Most folks make the switch in a single meeting, any time of year.

Do you work year-round or only at tax time?

Year-round. Bookkeeping, payroll, and quarterly planning don't stop in April, and neither do we. You'll have someone to call in any season.

I use QuickBooks / spreadsheets / a shoebox — does that work for you?

All of the above. Bring what you've got. We'll meet you where you are and tidy up whatever needs tidying.

What should I bring to a first consultation?

Last year's tax return, recent bank or bookkeeping statements, and your questions. If you're not sure, just bring what you have — we'll tell you what's missing.

How are your fees set?

Fairly and up front. We'll give you a clear estimate before any work begins, so there are no surprises on the invoice.
